The History of Nintendo

  • 1889: Nintendo is founded as a playing card company.
  • 1929: Sekiryo Kaneda becomes company president.
  • Hiroshi Yamauchi starts transforming Nintendo into a toy company.
  • 1956: Nintendo explores U.S. markets and begins diversification.
  • 1963: Nintendo Co., Ltd. is established and expands into toys and electronics.
  • 1970: Nintendo enters the electronic toy market.
  • 1977: Release of the Color TV‑Game series.
  • 1980: Launch of Game & Watch.
  • 1981: Release of Donkey Kong.
  • Launch of the Famicom / NES.
  • 1985: Worldwide NES release and Super Mario Bros.
  • 1989: Release of the Game Boy.
  • 1990: Launch of the Super Nintendo (SNES).
  • 1996: Release of the Nintendo 64.
  • 1998: Launch of the Game Boy Color.
  • 2001: Release of the Game Boy Advance and GameCube.
  • 2004: Launch of the Nintendo DS.
  • 2006: Release of the Nintendo Wii, becoming one of the best‑selling consoles of all time.
  • 2011: Launch of the Nintendo 3DS
  • 2012: Release of the Wii U.
  • 2017: Launch of the Nintendo Switch.
  • 2019: Release of the Switch Lite.
  • 2021: Release of the Switch OLED
♦️ Fun Fact: Nintendo started in 1889 making hand-painted playing cards.
